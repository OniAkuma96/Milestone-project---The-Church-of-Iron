About Us

Opening Times

We are open 7 days a week from 6am-11pm. Please check our contact page for questions or queries on our opening hours.

History

Founded in 2010 by John Wellington, with the simple goal of providing the local area of West London with their fitness needs, Religious Fitness quickly established itself as a local favourite. In 2013 we expanded our rooms allowing us to offer classes to our members and more space for all the latest and greatest equipment for all your fitness needs.

Our Equipment

Religious Fitness features all the latest and greatest in exercise machines. Whatever muscle you want to work out, we got a machine for it. As for cardio, from treadmills, ellipticals, rowing machines and cycling machines you can always find a machine open at Religious Fitness.

Our Classes

Our classes are run by experienced professionals who understand the ins and outs of human biology. We offer a range of classes so whatever your age or experience we guarantee there's a class for you. Our yoga class is great for anyone. Whether your looking to train your balance in your old age or just for a nice pre-workout warmup it's a great choice. Our cycling class is for those who want to test their fitness levels. Our trainers will only push you as hard as you want to be pushed. If you do want to be pushed, our cycling class will push you for sure! Lastly our kickboxing class is for our toughest members. Think you got what it takes? Not for the feint of heart!
